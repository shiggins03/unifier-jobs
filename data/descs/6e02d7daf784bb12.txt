About the Position

We’re seeking a 
Document Control Manager
 to support large, complex infrastructure programs, including highways, roads, bridges, transit (rail and bus), international airports, and water/wastewater projects delivered through both traditional and alternative delivery methods. Your primary focus will be leading document control operations, information governance, and enterprise document management initiatives that support program delivery, regulatory compliance, and organizational performance across major capital programs. This role will oversee document control personnel, establish and maintain program-wide standards, and support implementation and optimization of Electronic Document Management Systems (EDMS). The position requires close collaboration with project teams, consultants, contractors, quality management personnel, information technology staff, and executive leadership.

This is a long-term, full-time, on-site position located in Sacramento, CA.

Responsibilities
 
may include, but are not limited to, the following:

Lead, manage, and continuously improve document control operations supporting large, complex infrastructure programs.

Supervise, mentor, and develop document control staff while establishing workload priorities and performance expectations.

Develop, implement, and maintain document control policies, procedures, standards, templates, and governance practices.

Oversee implementation, administration, optimization, and adoption of Electronic Document Management Systems (EDMS) and related document management technologies.

Coordinate document control activities among project teams, consultants, contractors, and stakeholders to ensure consistency and compliance.

Establish and monitor key performance indicators, dashboards, reporting tools, and program metrics to support executive decision-making.

Support business process improvement initiatives, workflow automation efforts, and operational efficiency enhancements.
