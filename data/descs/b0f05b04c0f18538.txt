About the Position

We’re seeking a 
Project Scheduler
 to support a portfolio of major transit capital improvement projects. Your primary focus will be developing, maintaining, and analyzing project schedules for the owner's Project Controls team, supporting projects from planning and design through construction and closeout while helping ensure successful project delivery.

This is a long-term, full-time, on-site position located in San Jose, CA.

Responsibilities
 
may include, but are not limited to, the following:

Develop, maintain, and update master schedules, detailed project schedules, schedule forecasts, recovery schedules, and schedule reports for a portfolio of transportation capital projects.

Manage and regularly update schedules for approximately 80 to 100 active projects throughout planning, design, procurement, and construction.

Analyze project schedules to identify critical path activities, schedule risks, constraints, and opportunities for schedule improvement.

Review contractor baseline schedules, monthly schedule updates, recovery schedules, and schedule narratives for compliance with contract requirements.

Perform Time Impact Analysis (TIA) to evaluate schedule impacts associated with contract changes, delays, and change orders.

Coordinate closely with project managers, project controls staff, consultants, contractors, and stakeholders to maintain accurate project schedules and resolve schedule-related issues.

Integrate individual project schedules into larger program or master schedules to support program-level reporting and decision-making.

Support project controls activities involving schedule performance, cost controls, funding, earned value concepts, and project reporting.

Prepare schedule analyses, progress reports, executive dashboards, and other management reports that communicate project status, schedule performance, and forecasted outcomes.
