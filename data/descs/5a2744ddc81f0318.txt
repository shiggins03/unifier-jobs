STV is seeking a Senior Project Controls Specialist to support the Project Controls Department in our Fairfax, VA / Washington, DC region offices. This role is designed for a highly experienced project controls professional who operates at a lead level, bridging the gap between Project Controls Specialist and Project Controls Manager.
The ideal candidate will serve as a technical authority and trusted advisor to project teams and clients—responsible for overseeing cost, schedule, forecasting, and change management while providing functional leadership to other project controls staff. This position does not require direct people management, but it does require strong leadership, influence, and ownership of project controls outcomes.
Key Responsibilities
Project Controls Leadership

Serve as the primary project controls point of contact for assigned projects or programs, working closely with Project Managers, Construction Managers, and client representatives.
Provide technical direction and mentorship to Project Controls Specialists and junior staff assigned to the project.
