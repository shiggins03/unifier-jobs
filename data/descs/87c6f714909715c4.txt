About the Position

We’re seeking a 
Claims and Change Specialist
 to support large, heavy-civil infrastructure programs, including highways, roads, bridges, transit (both rail and bus), international airports, and water/wastewater projects delivered through both traditional and alternative methods. Your primary focus will be supporting claims analysis, contract administration, and change management activities associated with complex infrastructure construction projects, including contract research, change control, commercial management support, and coordination with project controls and construction management teams.

This is a long-term, full-time, on-site position located in New York, NY.

Responsibilities
 
may include, but are not limited to, the following:

Support the review of contractor notices, change requests, contract modifications, and related documentation through contract research and claims analysis.

Assist in evaluating entitlement considerations by reviewing contract requirements, technical documentation, project records, and applicable procedures.

Draft correspondence related to contract changes, requests for clarification, and change management activities for management review.

Prepare written summaries, analyses, and supporting documentation outlining contractual considerations, findings, and key project issues.

Support administration of the change control process in accordance with contract requirements, project procedures, and established change management practices.

Review and track change requests to help ensure compliance with contractual requirements, documentation standards, and established timelines.

Compile, maintain, and organize supporting documentation associated with change requests, including cost, schedule, technical, and project controls information.

Maintain accurate records of change orders, approvals, correspondence, negotiations, and supporting documentation within project tracking systems and change logs.
