About this Role: 
The Unifier Solution Architect designs and leads the delivery of Oracle Primavera Unifier solutions that replace manual, paper, and spreadsheet-based processes with a governed, scalable system of record. This role partners with business and project stakeholders to define requirements, translate them into Unifier configurations, and ensure solutions are usable, maintainable, and aligned to program controls and governance.
Key Responsibilities:
Lead the design of Unifier and other PMIS solutions 
Work directly with clients to gather requirements, make recommendations, take detailed notes, and convey those to other members of the project team 
Create strategy documents, such as integration strategy, PMIS strategy, training strategy, etc
Create detailed design documentation, and artifacts
Work with developers and development leads to manage Unifier/PMIS development
Be able to create proof of concepts to demonstrate key functionality
Quality assurance on solutions developed by digital teams
Execute UAT process and obtain final sign off from clients
Assist with the development of integration and migration plans 
Escalate any issues promptly to project leadership
Demo solutions to clients 
Ability to manage shifting priorities and adapt quickly 
Support sales process as needed
Required Skills and Experience: 
5+ Years Experience with Oracle Primavera Unifier or similar PMIS
3+ Years Solution Architecture experience with Unifier or similar PMIS
